Design Philosophy

Classical Elegance: Rich golds and warm tones evoke illuminated manuscripts and Renaissance artistry.

Modern Sophistication: Deep blacks and subtle gradients provide contemporary dark mode comfort.

Renaissance Red Accents: Muted burgundy and venetian red tones add warmth and sophistication, reminiscent of rich tapestries and noble colors.

Scholarly Authority: Typography and spacing reflect the gravitas of academic tradition.

Digital Harmony: Colors designed for optimal screen readability and accessibility.
